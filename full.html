  • Career Compass App (Sydney Dews and Chad Wangolo) - In Progress

    • The application is a subscription-based platform designed to support individuals as they transition out of rehabilitation programs and back into society. It focuses on providing tools and resources to help users secure employment and build professional networks. The app will guide users through updating their resumes, finding references, taking professional headshots, and creating LinkedIn profiles to establish their professional presence. It will include templates for messaging employers, posting accomplishments, and tracking job applications through a spreadsheet system. To foster accountability and encouragement, users can pair up with fellow rehab graduates through a buddy system. The app will also offer virtual job counseling sessions and include a donation feature where the public can contribute professional clothing or funds to help participants dress for success. Rehabs will fund the first year of access for users, with an option to continue for a small monthly fee. Ultimately, the goal is to help individuals secure quality jobs, avoid relapse, and potentially give back by supporting other users or working part-time for the app.
